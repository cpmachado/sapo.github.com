SAPO Broker is a high-performance distributed messaging framework with minimal administration overhead, Publish-Subscribe and Point-to-Point messaging, guaranteed delivery and wildcard subscriptions. Client libraries are available for Perl, Python, PHP, .NET and C (among others).
